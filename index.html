About the Course:

To highlight our students' creative ingenuity and drive to generate and develop ideas for societal advancement, TISD was included to the new curriculum at FrCrce, Bandra. These concepts, which will eventually be turned into finished goods, support the SDGs of the UN and aim to address a number of issues that affect our daily lives.

SwiftScan

Members: Hruday Patil, Michelle Aloz, Anastasia Lopes, Vashni Nadar, Nick Pereira

Mentor: Prof. Prajakta Dhamnaskar

SGDs: 12 ,13 14.

SwiftScan is a facial recognition system designed to assist train staff in real-time by identifying passengers and tracking their locations. The core purpose of the system is highlighted: helping railway officials efficiently monitor passenger activity to reduce ticketless travel.

Carbon Counter

Members: Rohan Benegal, Reniyas Nadar, Chris Lopes, Russel Paul, Harsh Dalvi

Mentor: Prof. Sujata Deshmukh

SGDs: 1, 5, 8.

Carbon Counter is a real-time carbon footprint calculator designed to help individuals and businesses monitor their environmental impact. The core purpose of the system is to provide actionable insights and track emissions across daily activities, enabling users to make informed decisions and reduce their carbon footprint effectively.
